Hello, I’m
Rose Chang.

I’m a senior researcher at ETRI (Mi Chang, 장미). I study how people interact with autonomous vehicles and robots, turning eye-tracking, physiological signals, and machine learning into safer, more humane mobility experiences.

Research

Three threads of my research

Human-centered AI

Turning eye-tracking, physiological signals, and behavioral data into machine-learning models that understand and predict user experience.

ESWA · DSS · AEI · PLOS ONE

Interaction Design

Designing how people meet products and robots — from Eclipse, an ambient light, and Coolmet, a cooling helmet, to human–robot interaction at ETRI.

Dyson Award · KSDS · HCI Korea

Autonomous Vehicle & Mobility

Driver readiness, take-over safety, and passenger emotion in automated driving — quantified for safer conditionally automated vehicles.

SIGGRAPH · CHI · ISMAR
